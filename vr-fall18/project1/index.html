Project #1: Eat It

The theme of my project is the concept of conscious eating. There is a cow on top of the milk to make you feel guilty about drinking milk, honey by the Cheerios but also a bee above it make you feel guilty if you want add honey. The chicken on the placemat is there to judge you for having any egg in your meal. The box of 'Woke Oats' are meant to look guilt safe, but are also incredibly dull. Currently, there are no interactions between objects. The targets are just displayed with their proper graphics and the scenes surrounding them.
